About Us

       Lemery Colleges is one of the performing tertiary institutions on the first district of Batangas. It was established on 1994, and founded by Mr. Oscarlito M. Hernandez together with his wife Mrs. Filomena Hernandez. It is located at Brgy. Bagong Sikat, Lemery, Batangas. Lemery Colleges offers different college courses and Junior High School. But because of the new curriculum kindergarten to Grade 12, also known as K-12, Lemery Colleges is also now offering Senior High School.

       At present, Lemery Colleges continues to achieve continuous increase in enrollment. Lemery Colleges were recognized to operate degree courses and has the ability to sustain the growth it has achieved in the past and will continue to strive in the coming years.


History

       On July 28, 1993, a young couple Oscarlito M. Hernandez and Filomena M. Hernandez founded the LEMERY COMPUTER CENTER and started its operation at the ground floor of R. De Villa Building, Ilustre Ave., Lemery, Batangas and offered tutorial courses. Due to positive feedback and encouragement from the different clientele, the couple founded LEMERY COMPUTER SCHOOL and occupied Parco Building at Rizal St. Lemery, Batangas and operate three (3) Associate Computer Courses namely: 2 years Computer Hardware Technology (CHT), 2 years Computer System Management (CSM) and 2 years Automated Office Management.

       To assure steady supply of enrollees, LEMERY COMPUTER SCHOOL expanded its operation by serving as Computer Education Provider for four (4) private Secondary Schools by providing them computer units and qualified teaching staff. To achieve greater flexibility and more associate and baccalaureate courses, LEMERY COMPUTER SCHOOL amended the Articles of Incorporation from LEMERY COMPUTER SCHOOL to LEMERY COLLEGES.

      Lemery Colleges continued to expand its offerings, gaining approval from CHED for programs in fields like Business Administration, Tourism Management, Criminology, and Customs Administration. It also received permits from TESDA for several technical-vocational courses. Significant infrastructure development followed, including a four-story building completed in 2019, a Senior High School building, and a gymnasium/multi-purpose hall. Located in Lemery, Batangas, the institution has demonstrated sustained growth since its inception and aims to remain a strong educational presence in the future.
